Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your data from our system, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to support@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your username and the email address associated with your account in the body of the email.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm the deletion of your data via email.

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at support@example.com.
